## Short Introduction
FastShort AI is an AI-powered tool that turns text, scripts, or ideas into ready-to-post short videos for TikTok, Reels, and YouTube Shorts in minutes.

## Medium Introduction
FastShort AI is a short-form video generator that automatically transforms text or scripts into complete faceless videos with visuals, voice-over, captions, and platform-optimized formatting. It supports multiple styles, multi-language subtitles, and automated publishing workflows, allowing creators and marketers to produce high-volume social content quickly without editing skills.
